About us

Marcela Malinova

Marcela has been working in the travel business since 1989. She had started as travel consultant for Spanish speaking countries. After a study experience in NYC's receptive company she started to work with Scandinavian and UK clients and she has enjoyed working with them until today. She likes to travel, visit new places and gather new experiences.

Filip Bis
